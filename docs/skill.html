WHAT IS SKILLLENS
A career preparation platform that tells students exactly what to fix
Most resume tools just check if a skill keyword exists in your resume. SkillLens goes deeper — it measures how well you actually know each skill, then maps that depth against 25 real Indian companies and tells you exactly which skills are costing you match percentage, and in what order to fix them.

One line explanation: SkillLens is a skill-depth measurement system that connects your current knowledge level to real company requirements and generates a personalised week-by-week plan to close the gap.

THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE
Resume tools only detect skill presence, not depth
Students don't know which company to realistically target
Mock interviews give a score — not specific feedback
No integrated system connects resume → quiz → companies → roadmap
WHAT MAKES IT UNIQUE
Skill depth quiz — measures actual knowledge, not keywords
Weighted company matching — 25 real Indian companies
Dual AI scoring — technical + communication separately
Resource-linked roadmap — real links, not generic advice
